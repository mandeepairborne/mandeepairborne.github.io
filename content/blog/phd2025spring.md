## Key to Short Forms

- **JRF**: Junior Research Fellowship  
- **GATE**: Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering  
- **JEST**: Joint Entrance Screening Test  
- **NET**: National Eligibility Test  
- **TBD**: To Be Decided/Updated  

---

## Important Notes  

1. **Verification Required**: All information, especially deadlines and eligibility criteria, is subject to change without notice. Prospective applicants should **always** verify current requirements directly from the official institutional websites.

2. **Application Cycles**: Many institutes have distinct application cycles for different academic sessions. Some may have already updated their admission processes for 2025-2026. Please check the official portals for the most current information.

3. **Rolling Admissions**: Several institutions accept applications throughout the year or have multiple admission cycles. Contact the respective departments for specific details.

4. **Exam Requirements**: While most institutes accept GATE/JRF/JEST scores, specific departments may have additional or different requirements. Always check department-specific eligibility criteria.

5. **Fee Structure**: Application fees mentioned are approximate and may vary based on category (General/SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD) and nationality (Indian/Foreign).
